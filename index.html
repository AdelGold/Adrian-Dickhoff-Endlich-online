"Xasthur


Editorial about the californian musician Scott Conner alias “Malefic” formerly known as “Xasthur” .

The Adventures of Cunibert and Reginald


A comic about an Insecure Knight, drawn in thick black lines and his silent compagnion Reginald the horse. Trust into a rich and complex World he doesn't understand desperatley trying to find meaning and purpose. The World is detailed, drawn with a thinner line to emphasize Cuniberts feeling of not belonging.
